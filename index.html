Kathiyawadi Cuisine


Aavkar is a new Kathiwadi restaurant located in the heart of the city. We specialize in authentic Kathiwadi cuisine prepared with the freshest ingredients. Our menu includes a variety of dishes from across the state, from the traditional to the contemporary. Our chefs use traditional methods and techniques to provide you with delicious flavors that will tantalize your taste buds.


Coming to kathiawad , its a peninsula in gujarat which also known as sourashtra cuisine.In this region there is high count of vegetarian and they are known for making spicy food. The most popular dishes here are bhakri's,eggplant curries,stuffed chillies, undhiyo, patra, dhokla, dhokli and gathiya.


The largest city of Gujarat and the former capital of the state, Ahmedabad is a gastronome's delight. This is where the supremely popular Gujarati Thali was served for the first time—way back in 1900 at the Chandravilas restaurant, which still continues to delight food lovers.
